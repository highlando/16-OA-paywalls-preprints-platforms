Ask a search engine...

... like google scholar to find a related preprint/postprint:

  • on an institutional preprint server, like our MPI preprint server
  • on Arxive
  • on the author's homepage

Ask a colleague or the public...

... like

  • the twitter community with the hashtag #icanhazpdf
  • the researchgate community
  • or the sci-hub project

Ask the author ...

... via a good old email

... or your librarian ...

How to help Open Access

Just do what would help you:

  • Put a preprint of your publications
    • on your institutional preprint server
    • or on Arxiv
  • Put a postprint of your publication
    • on your personal homepage
    • on your library's homepage
    • ... provided your are allowed to do so
    • ... ... (but typically you are)
  • or check the scientific networks
